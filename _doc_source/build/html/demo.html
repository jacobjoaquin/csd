Demos¶

These are examples of what can be built with the score module.

Demo scripts are located in /score/demo.

Note

Demos have only been tested using Apple’s Python 2.5.1. Further testing will need to be done in other versions of Python.

Warning

These are to be considered unstable releases until they are properly tested and bug checked.

align¶

Aligns groups of i-events.

-i Pad amount between statement and identifier¶
-p Pad amount between pfields 1 and higher¶
-c Pad amount between last pfield and trailing comment¶
-m Minimum pad amount between pfields 1 and higher¶

Example:

$ cat unfactored.sco | ./align.py -m4

Before:

i1 0 4 6.04 15000 2 100 81 50 56 20 1 ; No alignment
i1 + . . . . 101 . . . . . 
i1 + . 5.04 . . 100 . . . . . 
i1 + . . . . 101 . . . . . 
i1 + . 6.04 . 2 100 . . . . . 
i1 + . . . . 101 . . . . . 
i1 + . 5.04 . . 100 . . . . . 
i1 + . . . . 101 . . . . .

arpeggiator¶

Arpeggiates values in for a selected pfield column for a specific event.

This script can be used as command-line script as well as a module.

-s Statement (required)¶
-i Identifier (required)¶
-p Pfield (required)¶
-v Values (required)¶

Example:

$ cat arp.sco | ./arpeggiator.py -si -i2 -p5 -v"7.00 7.03 7.07 7.10"

Before:

i 1 0 4    1.0 9.07
i 2 0 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
i 1 0 4    1.0 9.11

After:

i 1 0 4    1.0 9.07
i 2 0 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + .    .   7.03
i 2 + .    .   7.07
i 2 + .    .   7.10
i 2 + .    .   7.00
i 2 + .    .   7.03
i 2 + .    .   7.07
i 2 + .    .   7.10
i 2 + .    .   7.00
i 2 + .    .   7.03
i 1 0 4    1.0 9.11

Arpeggiates values in for a selected pfield column for a specific event.

carry¶

Replaces subsequent repeated values with a carry (.)

This script can be used as command-line script as well as a module.

Example:

$ cat carry.sco | ./carry.py | ./align.py

Before:

i 2 0 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.3 7.00
/**/
i 2 + 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 0 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00

After:

i 2 0 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + .    .   .
i 2 + .    .   .
/**/
i 2 + .    0.5 .
i 2 + .    0.3 .
i 2 + .    0.5 .
i 1 0 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 .    .   .
i 1 1 .    .   .
i 1 1 .    .   .
carry.replace(s)¶
Replaces subsequent repeated values with a carry (.)

sum¶

Sums each pfield in an column with a user-specified value.

This script can be used as command-line script as well as a module.

-s Statement (required)¶
-i Identifier (required)¶
-p Pfield (required)¶
-v Value (required)¶

Example:

$ cat carry.sco | ./sum.py -si -i2 -p4 -v0.999

Before:

i 2 0 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.3 7.00
/**/
i 2 + 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.3 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 0 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00

After:

i 2 0 0.25 1.299 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 1.299 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 1.299 7.00
/**/
i 2 + 0.25 1.499 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 1.299 7.00
i 2 + 0.25 1.499 7.00
i 1 0 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00
i 1 1 0.25 0.5 7.00

swap_columns¶

Exchanges all score columns for a specified statement and identifier.

-s Statement (required)¶
-i Identifier (required)¶
-a Pfield A (required)¶
-b Pfield B (required)¶

The following command-line swaps pfields 4 and 5 for all instrument 1 events:

$ cat swap.sco | ./swap_columns.py -si -i1 -a4 -b5

Before:

i 1 0 4 6.04 15000 2 100 81 50 56 20 1
i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .
i 1 + . 6.02 5000  2 100 81 50 56 20 1
i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .
i 1 + . 6.01 8000  2 100 81 50 56 20 1
i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .
i 1 + . 6.09 11000 2 100 81 50 56 20 1
i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .

i 7 16.4 3.5 8000 5.019 0.2 0.7 0.5 2 3 0.1
i 7 19.3 6.4 8000 5.041 .   0.9 1   3 2 0.1

i 7 19.3 6.4 8000 5.041 .   0.9 1   3 2 0.1

After:

i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .
i 1 + . 5000 6.02  2 100 81 50 56 20 1
i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .
i 1 + . 8000 6.01  2 100 81 50 56 20 1
i 1 + . .    .     . 101 .  .  .  .  .
